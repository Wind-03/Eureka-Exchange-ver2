The Narrative Unfolded.

Research Commercialisation

The commercialization of research outcomes faces significant challenges, with success rates generally ranging between 5% and 20%.

Challenges including the need for refinement, limited funding, and the academia-industry gap hinder success rates, yet collaborative efforts and innovation support offer potential for improved commercialization outcomes.

Market Opportunity

There's a growing need for domain-specific models to unlock the value and achieve the projected global digital twin market size of $48.2 billion by 2026

Research Outcomes

Up to 80% of research outcomes are models which are archived in standalone computing devices and eventually discarded due to our century-old research publication strategy
